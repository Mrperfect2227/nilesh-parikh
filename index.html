Professional Profile

A decade of SMSF excellence
built offshore, delivered onshore.

Nilesh Parikh is a CMA-qualified SMSF professional who has spent over a decade at the intersection of Australian superannuation compliance, offshore delivery operations, and intelligent automation. From managing audit pipelines for thousands of funds annually to winning a hackathon for cloud-based audit automation, his career is defined by making large, complex operations run with precision.

Currently serving as Associate Director at SMSF Auditing Australia, Nilesh leads multi-location teams delivering 8,000–10,000 SMSF audits per year. His deep expertise spans BGL 360, Class Super, Cloudoffis, and SuperMate — the full spectrum of platforms trusted by Australia's top accounting firms.

What distinguishes Nilesh is not just technical fluency but leadership depth: he has scaled offshore operations from 20 to 40+ professionals, mentored senior auditors into leadership roles, and driven 20% annual cost savings through workflow automation — all while maintaining impeccable ATO and SIS Act compliance.
